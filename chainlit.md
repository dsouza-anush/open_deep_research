# Deep Research

Your AI-powered research assistant that conducts thorough, multi-source investigations on any topic.

---

## Research Modes

Choose the right mode for your needs:

| Mode | Best For | Depth |
|------|----------|-------|
| **Quick** | Simple questions, fact-checking | 1 iteration |
| **Standard** | General research, balanced depth | 2 iterations |
| **Deep** | Complex topics, detailed analysis | 3 iterations |
| **Expert** | Academic, professional research | 5 iterations |

## How It Works

1. **Ask** - Type your research question or select a starter
2. **Configure** - Adjust settings for depth and search preferences
3. **Research** - Watch as multiple AI researchers investigate your topic
4. **Review** - Get a comprehensive, well-sourced report

## Tips for Better Results

- **Be specific** - Include context, timeframes, or constraints
- **Ask follow-ups** - Dive deeper into interesting findings
- **Use Expert mode** - For thorough academic-style research
- **Check sources** - All findings include citations you can verify
